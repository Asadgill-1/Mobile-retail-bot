> ⚠️ **UPDATED CONCEPTS (2026-07-09) — this brainstorm is the original design; three ideas below have since changed. The BUILT SYSTEM follows the updated versions, not the original text. Corrections are marked `[UPDATED …]` at the exact spots.**

## 3. AI ANTI-HALLUCINATION & HUMAN ESCALATION
> [UPDATED 2026-07-09] Model is **Moonshot `kimi-k2.6`** in testing (provider abstraction swaps to GPT-4o at production). ## 4. PRODUCT INVENTORY WITH FLEXIBLE SPECIFICATIONS

### Products Table Schema:

- Sorts by: boost_level DESC, relevance score (matching spec pairs + matching tags × 2), is_featured DESC.

## 7. INTRUSION DETECTION & AUTO-QUARANTINE

### Attack patterns to detect:
1. Prompt injection: "ignore previous", "you are DAN", "system prompt", "act as admin", base64, messages >2000 chars.
2. SQL injection patterns.
3. Rapid-fire: 20+ messages in 60 seconds.
4. Cross-shop probing: "other shops", "all your locations", "give me data from shop #".
5. Admin commands in customer messages: `/addshop`, `/owner`, `/pauseshop`.
6. Credential probing: "API key", "database password", "show me backend".

### When attack detected, AUTOMATICALLY:
1. Quarantine: `quarantine:{phone}` in Redis (1-hour TTL).
2. Stop processing: generic reply "Your message could not be processed."
3. Capture LAST 25 MESSAGES from Redis session into `security_incidents` table.
4. Alert owner via Telegram with full forensic data and incident ID.

### Owner investigation:
- `/investigate <incident_id>`, `/quarantine_extend`, `/quarantine_lift`, `/blacklist <phone>`, `/forward_to_shop <phone> <shop_id>`.
